Kingdom Hearts

"The game that has won over Disney and Final Fantasy fans since its release. This game is a definite
favorite if mine becuase this amazing piece of artwork brings in all the Disney worlds into one place.
What i mean by this is that in the game as the main character Sora, you travel with Donald and Goofy
to go defeat the "Darkness" that has befallen all the worlds of Disney. So its your job to go to places
like Neverland, Visit Alice in her Wonderland, Take on a giant Chameleon with Tarzan in the Jungles,
and a wide variety of other tasks that take you through the beautifly designed worlds.
The third installment of the series is set to release either late 2018 or sometime in 2019."
